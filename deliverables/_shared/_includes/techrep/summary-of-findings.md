## Summary of findings

The 18 methods partition into four epistemic roles:

| Signal | Methods | Pattern |
|--------|---------|---------|
| **Field convergence** | S1–S4, L1, L2, G2, G9 | Peak Z ≈ +2–4 around 1998–2002; monotonic decline thereafter. Early climate finance was heterogeneous; the field homogenised over time. |
| **Structural break (topology)** | G5, G6 | Peak Z ≈ +2.4–3 at **2007**. Citation network topology — preferential attachment exponent and degree entropy — shifted sharply, consistent with the post-Stern/UNFCCC-Bali institutionalisation of climate finance. |
| **Reference / sanity check** | C2ST | Classifier AUC sits at 0.60–0.67 throughout — above chance in every year. Confirms that the distance-based methods are not measuring noise; does not vote on zones. |
| **Noisy / weak / cumulative** | G1, G3, G4, G7, G8, L3 | Weak or non-directional signal (G1 peak Z=1.3); L3 returns a term-level list rather than a scalar divergence. Useful as robustness checks and lexical fingerprints. |
